About me

I'm a Lead Data Engineer who turns fragmented source systems into centralized, trusted data platforms — owning the full lifecycle from ingestion and transformation to modeling, orchestration, and the governance that keeps it reliable.

I've delivered across Microsoft Azure & Fabric, Google Cloud, AWS, and Snowflake, building everything from near real-time streaming pipelines to medallion lakehouse architectures powering executive Power BI reporting.
